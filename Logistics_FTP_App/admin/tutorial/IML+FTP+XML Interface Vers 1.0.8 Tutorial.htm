User Authentication Tutorial:

 

This is the initial prompt the users will see when entering the IML/FTP/XML Interface Version 1.0.8 for the first time after Friday 10-28-2005.

 

Do NOT be alarmed !

 

Any user who wishes to have a user account for this application can easily obtain one WITHOUT the need to go through the I.T. Dept.

 

 

First, choose the link called “register for a new user account” as shown below:

 

 

Then click on this link and proceed to the next step in the process.

 

 

At this point you will want to enter your redacted email address along with a Secret Prompt and Secret Response as shown below:  (The Secret Prompt and Secret Response should be something that means something to you personally and can be anything you desire.  Keep in mind the Secret Prompt and Secret Response are both stored in the database in an encrypted tamper-proof manner however others may be able to view your Secret Prompt while using the system such as whenever someone attempts to use another person’s email address when attempting to log-in.)

 

 

Click the [Submit] button to proceed to the next step in the process.

 

At this point, as shown below, your new user account has been established in the database however you will need to Activate your new account.

 

 

New User Account Activation is performed via the email you will receive that has a link (as shown below) which you will click on to Activate your new user account.

 

This New User Account Activation process ensures that nobody but you can spoof the system into thinking they are you when in fact only you are you.

 

The only time you will ever need to contact the I.T. Dept is in the event that you don’t receive the email.

 

 

At this time you should click on the link as shown below:

 

 

And then you will see the following pop-up in a browser window:

 

 

Your password will be emailed to you at this time.

 

At this time you should return to the IML/FTP/XML Interface Version 1.0.8 home page where you will see the following:

 

 

At this time you may login to begin working OR you may have your password reset.

 

There are two ways to get your password reset once your new user account has been activated.

 

Notice the link labeled “forgot your password” – this link is used whenever you forgot your password and you wish to have a new password issued by the system.

 

 

Go ahead and click this link now, nothing bad will happen.  In fact you may click this link as often as you wish and the system will cheerfully issue you a new password each time you click on this link as shown below:

 

 

At this point you should enter your redacted email address as shown below:

 

 

Notice that the Secret Prompt you entered before is shown to you again here.  This is how the system knows that you are really who you say you are because only YOU know the correct Secret response to enter.  I’ll enter mine so we can proceed with this Tutorial as shown below:

 

 

At this time your new password has been sent to your email address so you can log-in and resume working.

Click on the link labeled “change your password” to begin the process of changing your password as shown below:

 

 

At this point you should enter your redacted email address and your old and new password and then click the [Submit] button to proceed.  I have done this as shown below to demonstrate:

 

 

At this point you should enter the Secret Response for the Secret Prompt and then click the [Submit] button as shown below:

 

 

At this point you will receive an email with the new password you just entered as a reminder and confirmation of the password you wanted your new password to be.

 

Keep in mind that you can, at any time, choose to have a system generated password issued in place of any password simply by using the Forgot Password procedure as shown above.
